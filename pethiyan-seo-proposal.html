Executive Summary
Within a ₹30,000 one-time engagement, this proposal covers all high-ROI fixes: technical SEO audit & schema markup (both complimentary), all admin panel & frontend SEO system updates (free of cost), keyword mapping, on-page optimisation, and Google Business Profile setup — giving the site a complete SEO foundation to build on.
Identified SEO Gaps
HIGH No Schema Markup

Zero structured data on the site. No Product, Organization, FAQ, BreadcrumbList, or LocalBusiness schema — means no rich snippets in Google results.

HIGH Missing Meta Descriptions

Homepage and most category/product pages lack meta descriptions, directly reducing click-through rates from search results.

HIGH Weak H1 / On-Page Copy

H1 is brand-generic ("Pethiyan – Packaging Products Supplier in India"). Category pages have no body copy — Google has nothing to rank.

HIGH Google Business Profile

No verified GBP listing found. Critical for "packaging supplier near me" and city-based searches which carry high commercial intent.

MED No Geo-Targeted Pages

Zero city-specific landing pages (Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, etc.) despite being an India-wide supplier. High-intent local searches are being missed entirely.

MED Unoptimised Image Alt Texts

Product images lack descriptive alt text, losing image-search traffic and hurting accessibility signals.

MED No Internal Linking Strategy

Blog posts do not link to related product or category pages. Pagerank flows out of content without benefiting commercial pages.

MED FAQ Page Not Schema-Marked

FAQ exists in navigation but has no FAQ schema — missing the "People Also Ask" rich result opportunity in SERPs.
